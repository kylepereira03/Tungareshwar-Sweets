Our Story

Founded in 1995, Tungareshwar has been delighting customers with authentic Indian sweets and snacks for over 25 years. What started as a small family shop has grown into a beloved destination for traditional delicacies in Vasai.

Our commitment to quality and authentic taste has earned us a special place in our customers' hearts, making us a part of their celebrations and everyday moments.
